description=A mod presented by the Coco Labs community focusing on increasing the possibilities with the game liquids. Store liquid in a "Water Jug" and "Large Gas Can". Take and Place Water Jugs on any dispenser to refill them.

description=[h1]New Feature:[/h1]

description=- Add/Siphon gasoline to/from vehicle with any fuel containers.
description=- Multiple translation now available.

description=[hr]
description=
description=[h1]Feature:[/h1]
description=- 3D Models.
description=- Pour fuel on the ground.
description=- Vanilla Gas Can (Store Fuel/Water).
description=- Large Gas Can (Store Fuel/Water).
description=- Water Jug (Take/Place on dispenser & store water/fuel).
description=- Replaceable Water Dispenser Bottles (Water/Fuel/Empty).
description=- Dynamic Water Dispenser (Change to empty bottle when depleted).
description=- Choose fuel container in Gas Pump Context menu.
description=- Choose fuel container in Generator Context menu.
description=- Add/Siphon Fuel with any fuel container.
description=- View any liquid container's content in the Context menu.
